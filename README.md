# Telecom Customer Churn Analytics

## Overview

The Telecom Customer Churn Analytics project is an end-to-end business intelligence and customer analytics solution designed to identify customer churn patterns, retention risks, and business opportunities using SQL, Python, and Power BI. The project focuses on transforming raw telecom customer data into actionable business insights through data analysis, visualization, and predictive analytics.

This project demonstrates practical experience in customer analytics, churn analysis, KPI reporting, business storytelling, and predictive modeling — key areas used in real-world analytics and data science roles.

---

# Business Problem

Telecom companies face significant revenue loss due to customer churn. Understanding why customers leave and identifying high-risk customers is critical for improving customer retention and maximizing long-term revenue.

The objective of this project was to:

* Analyze customer behavior and churn trends
* Identify key churn drivers
* Segment high-risk customers
* Build interactive dashboards for business monitoring
* Support retention-focused business decisions using predictive analytics

---

# Project Objectives

* Perform exploratory data analysis on telecom customer datasets
* Analyze customer churn behavior using SQL and Python
* Identify retention risks and high-risk customer segments
* Build Power BI dashboards for KPI monitoring and business storytelling
* Develop a Logistic Regression model to predict customer churn probability
* Deliver actionable recommendations to improve customer retention

# Predictive Analytics

A Logistic Regression model was developed using Scikit-learn to identify high-risk churn customers and support retention-focused business decisions.

### Model Objectives

* Predict customer churn probability
* Identify key churn-driving features
* Support proactive retention strategies

### Key Insights

The predictive model highlighted that:

* Contract type
* Customer tenure
* Internet service category
* Monthly charges
* Payment method

were major factors influencing customer churn.

# Business Insights

### Key Findings

* Month-to-month customers had the highest churn probability
* Customers with low tenure were more likely to leave
* Fiber optic internet users showed higher churn trends
* Electronic payment users had increased churn risk
* Long-term contract customers demonstrated better retention rates
